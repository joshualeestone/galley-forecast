How It Works

Four Steps to Reader Intelligence

Upload your manuscript. We simulate the audience. You get the intelligence — and a playbook to act on it.

1

Upload Your Manuscript

Upload an EPUB, PDF, or Word document. Our system extracts themes, narrative structure, character dynamics, and genre signals — then immediately destroys the source file.

2

Simulate Your Audience

Thousands of AI reader personas — each with unique tastes, reading habits, and review styles — independently react to your book. Then they react to each other, creating authentic social dynamics.

3

Receive Your Forecast

Get a comprehensive intelligence report: predicted ratings, simulated social posts, influencer matches, launch recommendations, and specific manuscript feedback — all within 24 hours.

4

Act on Your Playbook

We don't just hand you data — we tell you exactly what to do next. Specific manuscript adjustments, influencer outreach strategy, optimal launch timing, and marketing angle recommendations tailored to your book's unique strengths.
