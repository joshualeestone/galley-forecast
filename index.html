How It Works

Four Steps to Reader Intelligence

Upload your manuscript. We simulate the audience. You get the intelligence — and a playbook to act on it.

1

Upload Your Manuscript

Upload an EPUB, PDF, or Word document. We analyze narrative structure, themes, pacing, character dynamics, and genre signals — then destroy the source file after processing.

2

Simulate Your Audience

We generate a large synthetic audience modeled around different reading tastes, review styles, and platform behaviors to forecast likely reception patterns.

3

Receive Your Forecast

Get a comprehensive intelligence report: predicted ratings, simulated social posts, influencer matches, launch recommendations, and specific manuscript feedback — all within 24 hours.

4

Act on Your Playbook

You receive a practical playbook with recommended positioning, influencer targets, launch angles, and manuscript considerations based on your forecast.

Data Protection

Your manuscript is processed, then permanently destroyed. No copies, no backups, no exceptions.

Your content is never used to train, fine-tune, or improve any AI model. We use API providers with contractual no-training guarantees.
